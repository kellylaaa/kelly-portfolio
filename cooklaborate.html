Meal Swapping App
Providing meal variety with meal prepping through meal swapping
Role: UX Designer and Researcher
Tools: Figma and Google Sheets
Duration: January - March 2024
Overview
PROBLEM
People lack variety in their daily meals
People often don't have time to cook for themselves every day or struggle with finding new and easy recipes to make. As a result, they either resort to ordering takeout, meal prepping in bulk, or cooking the same meals everyday. The first option is costly and also tends to be unhealthy. The second and last option, while it is cheaper and more nutritious, eating the same few meals for several days at a time easily can get repetitive and boring.
Goal
Provide meal variety without the extra work
CookLaborate is a platform to organize events for people in their community to exchange meals that they have prepped in advance. The planning and cooking will take place asynchronously online. Then people will agree on a meet-up time and location to exchange their prepped meals, try it out, leave any feedback, and even recreate it themselves if they want!
